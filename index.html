Info

  • The goal of each game round is to get 20 points.
  • A card is drawn at the start of your turn.
  • You can play one card per turn from your hand.(Or cheat and do more)
  • Blue cards increase your points. Orange ones decrease.
  • Shift click a white border card to flip it's operator: (+ > - & - > +)
  • Click stand when you have 20 points (or less if feeling lucky)
  • End Turn to move the game along (and get a new card next turn)
  • Who ever wins 3 rounds wins the game!
  • May the force be with you.
